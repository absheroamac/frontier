## 1. WHAT IS FRONTIER?

Project Frontier is a **new-age AI-native startup sprint studio**.

It is not a traditional agency. It is not a dev shop. It is not a hackathon platform.

It sits at the intersection of:
- **Startup studio** (execution + strategy from Day Zero)
- **Craft guild** (vetted builders, taste-driven output)
- **Immersive experience** (offsite sprints, curated locations, focused builds)
- **Design + development studio** (interactive web, AI products, SaaS, mobile)

The one-line pitch:
> "The only thing standing between you and your billion-dollar startup idea is a weekend of commitment."

Founders walk in with an idea. They walk out with a validated MVP — built in 5 days or a weekend.

## 8. DESIGN LANGUAGE (Frontier Visual Identity)

Frontier interfaces feel like **products, not marketing pages**.

**Core aesthetic:** minimal, intelligent, engineered, immersive, calm, system-driven

**Color system — neutral-first, signal accents:**
- `#1E252A` — Carbon Black (primary dark surface)
- `#197853` — Deep Emerald
- `#A1463E` — Earth Red
- `#3972B4` — System Blue
- `#E26900` — Signal Orange
- `#0015FF` — Electric Blue
- `#00FFF1` — Neon Cyan
- Accent colors used for hover states, interactive signals, motion highlights only

**Typography:**
- Display: Helvetica Now Display (hero titles, section headlines)
- Interface: Geist (body, nav, UI labels, metadata)

**Geometric system:** Circles are the primary primitive — signals, nodes, systems, intersections. Large, thin-stroke, intentional. Never decorative.

**Motion:** Smooth, slow, controlled, cinematic. GSAP + Framer Motion + Three.js/WebGL.

**Scroll narrative:** Curiosity → Discovery → Understanding → System → Proof → Invitation
