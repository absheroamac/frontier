# Contact — Structure

## Purpose
A dedicated, global landing page to intake general project and agency inquiries for the Frontier team, separate from the Founder's personal channels.

## Sections (in order)
1. **Header**
   - Navigation: Standard Dark Mode global `Header` with Menu integration.
2. **General Inquiry Block**
   - Layout: Split 50/50 Grid (Matching the established `InquirySection` aesthetic, but full-screen scaling).
   - Content Left: "Reach out to the team." with general `hello@tryfrontier.com` routing.
   - Content Right: Standard Brutalist input form (Name, Email, Message, Submit).
   - Notes: Use the `bg-carbon` dark mode ecosystem for maximum gravitas.
3. **Footer**
   - Component: `MinimalFooter`

## Component Map
- `Header` (exists)
- `MinimalFooter` (exists)
- `ContactForm` (inline custom for this page to replace founder socials)

## Design Notes
Keep it stark, ultra-minimal, and purely utilitarian. Dark mode (`bg-carbon`). Form geometry will mimic the newly standardized transparent bottom-border primitives.
