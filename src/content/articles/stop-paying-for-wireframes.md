## The Figma Trap

Figma is phenomenal for establishing design systems and establishing visual direction. But spending 3 weeks mapping out static states that an engineer is going to have to manually interpret and recreate is an incredibly inefficient use of capital. 

> "Design the system, then deploy the code."
