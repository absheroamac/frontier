## The Core Loop

A Minimum Viable Product should do exactly one thing perfectly. If you are building a marketplace, the MVP isn't the profile pages, the slick onboarding flows, or the notification engine. The MVP is the transaction.

By aggressively cutting scope, a team can go from thesis to market testing in two weeks.
