<p class="lead text-2xl mb-12 font-medium">Users are significantly more forgiving of minor UX friction when the overall interface feels premium.</p>

## Human Psychology

We intuitively assign higher value and competence to things that look beautiful. Engineering a rock-solid backend means nothing if the frontend feels like it was assembled piece-meal. Visual harmony instills trust.
