## Compounding Speed

It is not simply that executing faster saves money; it fundamentally changes the nature of the product you build. Rapid iteration allows teams to course-correct based on live telemetry rather than theoretical whiteboard sessions.

> "The team that ships twice as fast learns four times as much."

The future stack isn't about writing code efficiently; it's about eliminating the code entirely where possible, and auto-generating the scaffolding so engineers only focus on the deeply custom, high-leverage business logic.
